WHY CHOOSE AFRICA SKILLS?

Africa Skills is a nationally recognised Occupational Education and Training Institute offering accredited Occupational qualifications since 2006. Our programmes are designed around one key goal: real skills for real jobs.

We partner with parents, schools, government, SETAs, and employers to deliver quality technical training across key industries including Business, Hospitality, Agriculture and Artisan Trade sectors. Our workplace-integrated model ensures graduates are not only certified but also job-ready. With campuses across South Africa and a graduate employment rate of 85%, Africa Skills stands out as one of the country's most trusted training providers for technical and practical Artisan Development.

ACCREDITED. RECOGNISED. TRUSTED.

Africa Skills is proud to be part of the UN Artisan Development Group. We are accredited by the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO) and multiple Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs), including MERSETA, AGRISETA, and CETA.

Our qualifications are compliant with national frameworks, recognised by employers across South Africa, and aligned with the latest workplace needs.
